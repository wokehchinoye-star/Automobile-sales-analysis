# 📊Automobile-sales-analysis-project overview

![Dashboard screenshot](Screenshots/dashboard.png)
## This project presents an interactive Automobile Sales Dashboard designed to analyze sales performance, profitability, customer demographics, and regional trends. The dashboard provides key insights to support data-driven decision-making in sales and product strategy.

## 🎯 Objectives
- Monitor total revenue, profit, cost, and quantity ordered
- Analyze customer revenue patterns by age group and gender
- Track sales performance across countries
- Identify high-profit product sub-categories
- Evaluate sales trends over time.

## 📈 Key Insights
- Total Revenue: 94.7M
- Total Profit: 32.0M

  - Average Unit Price: Bikes have significantly higher average unit prices compared to Clothings and Accessories
- Top Revenue Age Group: 26–35 years
- Highest Sales Region: United States
- Peak Revenue Year: 2015
- Top Profit Margin Sub-Category: Socks
- Gender Contribution: Sales are nearly balanced, with males slightly higher.

## 🛠️ Tools Used
- Tableau – Dashboard development and visualization
- Microsoft Excel – Data preparation and cleaning

## 📂 Dashboard Features
- KPI summary cards for quick performance overview
- Average unit price by category
- Revenue analysis by age group and gender
- Profit margin comparison by product sub-category

  - Revenue trend visualization across multiple years
- Country-level sales comparison

## 🚀 Outcome
This dashboard enables stakeholders to quickly identify growth opportunities, optimize product focus, and understand customer purchasing behavior across regions and demographics.
